Low-Carb Vegetarian Tomato Bake Recipe

The best Low-Carb Vegetarian Tomato Bake Recipe

This Low-Carb, vegetable packed bake recipe is super healthy and will give you all five of your daily portions of veg!

You can pack in all sorts of veg to this healthy Vegetarian recipe, we chose onion, mushrooms and peppers, and topped it all with mozarella cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • Various vegetables Eg. mushrooms, pepper, courgettes
  • 1 teaspoon oil
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 2 garlic cloves More or less to taste
  • 1 small onion
  • Cheese (optional)
  • 1 chopped chilli(optional) More or less to taste

How to make easy tomato vegetable bake

STEP 1

Chop up your veg: We used peppers and mushrooms. You could also use courgette and such.

STEP 2

Quarter the tomato, mince/chop up finely the garlic and chop up the chilli if using.

STEP 3

Once everything is ready to go, start by pre-heating the oven to 180 degress.

STEP 4

While that warms up, heat a teaspoon of oil in a frying pan over a medium heat.

STEP 5

Add the onions, garlic and chilli and fry for 5 minutes.

STEP 6

Add in the veg, minus the tomatoes, and cook for a further 5-10 minutes until tender and softened.
